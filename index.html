Quantum Mechanics: The Science of the Infinitely Small

"If you are not completely confused by quantum mechanics, you do not understand it." — John Wheeler

Quantum mechanics is a fundamental branch of physics that describes the behavior of matter and energy at atomic and subatomic scales. It introduces revolutionary concepts like superposition, wave-particle duality, and quantum entanglement.

Wave-Particle Duality

Particles can behave as both waves and particles depending on observation.

Quantum Entanglement

Particles remain connected even when separated by large distances.
